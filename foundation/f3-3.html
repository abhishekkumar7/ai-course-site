Foundation · F3.3

Co-intelligence: being the human in the loop

F3.3  ·  part of the Foundation track

Objective

Frame AI as a collaborator, not an oracle, using Mollick's practical rules for working with AI.

Key concepts

  • “Always invite AI to the table” and “be the human in the loop”
  • Treating AI as a smart but unreliable colleague
  • When to trust, when to verify, when to refuse
  • Augmentation vs. automation of a task

Hands-on

Pick one task from your week; redesign it as a human-AI workflow, deciding what AI drafts and what you must check.
