About this curriculum

Concepts you can act on, not just admire.

This is a public, link-rich curriculum that teaches AI from a management perspective, with a parallel research-methods track. It is deliberately tool-rich and math-light: read a short explainer, do the activity in a free browser tool, then follow the sources to go deeper.

How a lesson works. Every session is a single page with the same shape — Objective, Key concepts, a Hands-on activity, free Tools, and Read / explore links. Take one lesson, or stack a whole track.

Who it's for

Two audiences, one rigorous spine.

Managers · Consultants · Leaders

Build judgment, not models

You need to evaluate, adopt, and govern AI — not engineer it. The Management track is heavier on cases, ROI, risk, and change management, and lighter on mathematics. Grounded in Indian and global business contexts.

Academics · PhD students · Analysts

Use AI as a research instrument

You want AI in the research process and as a method itself. The Research track is heavier on methodology, validity, and reproducibility — the things that separate research use from casual use.
