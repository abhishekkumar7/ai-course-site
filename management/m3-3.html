Management · M3.3

From text and behaviour to intelligence

M3.3  ·  part of the Management track

Objective

Use NLP and pattern-mining to turn customer voice and behaviour into decisions.

Key concepts

  • Sentiment and topic modelling; classical NLP vs. LLM cost/complexity
  • Association and co-purchase patterns (cross-sell, assortment)
  • Actionable signal vs. noise
  • Semantic search and RAG for enterprise knowledge

Hands-on

Paste a set of customer reviews into a chatbot and extract themes, sentiment, and three actions; sanity-check the themes.
